Victor Gandarillas

Aerospace Engineer

Hello!

I am currently working as a Ph.D. student under professor John Hwang in the Large Scale Design Optimization Laboratory (LSDO lab) in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at the University of California San Diego.

My current research is in Multidisciplinary Design Optimization; using gradient-based optimization to solve large scale nonlinear programs (NLPs); i.e., problems with thousands of design variables. More recent work has involved automatic generation of numerical methods and spacecraft design and trajectory optimization (optimal control via direct transcription).

I obtained my Master of Science in Aerospace Engineering at the University of California San Diego under professor Jorge Cortes in the Multi-Agent Robotics (MURO) Lab in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering.

I completed my undergraduate work at Purdue University in the Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics.
